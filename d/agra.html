§ Why Agra works for solar

Agra gets 5.4 kWh/m²/day of solar irradiance.

That's enough sunlight to generate roughly 365 units of electricity per month from a 3 kW rooftop system — covering most household consumption in Agra. With grid tariffs at ₹6.50/unit on DVVNL, that translates to ₹2,555 in monthly bill savings.

As one of UP's more urbanized districts (45% urban), Agra sees most demand from residential rooftops and RWA group installations rather than farm pumps.
