§ 02 — THE SCHEMES

Four programs.
Stack them.

Most Indians only know about PM Surya Ghar. Depending on your state and use-case, you can stack 2-3 of these on the same system. Here's what each is actually worth.

01 / CENTRAL

PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana

The flagship. 1 crore household target by March 2027. Direct bank transfer after net meter install. ₹75,021 cr scheme outlay.

₹78,000
max for 3 kW+ residential
02 / STATE

UPNEDA State Top-up

UP's stacked state subsidy. ₹15,000/kW up to 2 kW. Auto-disbursed via PM Surya Ghar portal — no separate application.

₹30,000
on top of central subsidy
03 / FARMER

PM-KUSUM A · B · C

Off-grid pumps (B), grid-pump solarization (C), or 500kW-2MW solar plant on barren land (A). Phase I ends March 2026.

60%
central + state combined

Stamp duty exemption, 10-yr electricity duty waiver, no permission needed for under-4.5m roof structures, no electrical inspection up to 10 kW.
