Privacy Policy

Last updated · May 18, 2026

SolarSubsidies.com ("we", "us") is a service of Webworks Media Network. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we share it.

What We Collect

  • Calculator inputs: Your state, system size, monthly bill, property type. Used only to generate your subsidy estimate. Stored anonymously for aggregate analytics.
  • Lead form data: Name, phone, email, district (when you request quotes). Stored in our database to match you with vendors.
  • Usage data: Page views, clicks, session duration via PostHog/Google Analytics. Aggregated, non-identifying.

What We Don't Do

  • We never sell your data to third parties.
  • We never share your contact with more than 3 vetted, MNRE-empanelled vendors per request.
  • We don't install any solar systems ourselves — we have zero conflict of interest.

How Vendor Matching Works

When you submit a lead form, we share your name, phone, and city with up to 3 solar installers matched to your district. Those installers may contact you within 48 hours. If you don't want further contact, reply STOP to any message or email us at hello@solarsubsidies.com.

Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for vendor sharing
  • File a grievance with the Data Protection Board of India

Email privacy@solarsubsidies.com for any of the above. We respond within 7 working days.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for session management and analytics (PostHog, Google Analytics). No advertising cookies on calculator/result pages. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.
