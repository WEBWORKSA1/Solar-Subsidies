§ Step 1 of 6 · ~15 seconds

Do you own agricultural land?

KUSUM requires land ownership. Tenant farmers, lease-holders, and sharecroppers are not currently eligible directly (we'll explain alternatives if this applies to you).

§ Step 2 of 6 · ~20 seconds

How much land do you own?

Different KUSUM components have different land requirements. Component A needs 4+ acres; Component B works from 0.5 acres; Component C depends on your existing pump.

1 acre = ~0.4 hectares = 40 gunthas (in some UP areas: ~1.6 bigha)
